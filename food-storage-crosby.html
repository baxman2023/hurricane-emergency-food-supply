Frequently Asked Questions

What types of food should I store for hurricane emergencies?
Non-perishable items that are high in protein and calorie content, such as canned meat, dried beans, nuts, peanut butter, crackers and cereal.
How long can I store food for hurricane emergencies?
Most non-perishable items can be stored for up to two years without significant loss of nutritional value.
How do I store food for hurricane emergencies?
Store foods in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Vacuum sealing is also an effective method of storage.
Are there any special considerations when storing food for hurricane emergencies?
Make sure that all containers used to store the food are airtight and watertight. Consider rotating stocks regularly to ensure freshness.
