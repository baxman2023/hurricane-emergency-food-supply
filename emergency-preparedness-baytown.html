What type of food should I store for a hurricane emergency?

Where can I buy hurricane emergency food supplies in Baytown?

How long will hurricane emergency food supplies last?
Hurricane emergency food supplies will typically last up to two weeks depending on the type of food stored and how it is stored and preserved.
Are there any special instructions for storing hurricane emergency food supplies?
Yes, perishable items should be kept cold or frozen and non-perishable items should be kept dry and away from extreme temperatures.
