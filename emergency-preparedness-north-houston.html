Frequently Asked Questions

What emergency food supplies should I have on hand in case of a hurricane?
Non-perishable items such as canned goods, dried fruits and nuts, energy bars, ready-to-eat meals, and bottled water are essential for an emergency food supply.
Where can I find emergency food supplies in North Houston?
Grocery stores, convenience stores, and online retailers in the North Houston area typically carry a wide variety of emergency food supplies.
When should I start stocking up on emergency food supplies?
It is recommended to start building your emergency food supply at least one month before a hurricane is expected to hit your area.
How long will my emergency food supply last during a hurricane?
Depending on the size of your family and the amount of time you expect to be without power or access to grocery stores, your emergency food supply should last between 3-7 days.
