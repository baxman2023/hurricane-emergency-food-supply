Introduction


Intro: Having a hurricane survival kit is essential for any natural disaster. (It) will ensure that you are prepared with all the necessary items to stay safe! But, what should be included in your kit? One of the most important things is emergency food supplies!

Emergency food supplies provide nutrition and energy when other sources are not available. This could be anything from canned goods to ready-to-eat meals. If possible, try to include non-perishable items such as canned meats, fruits, veggies and soups. It's also recommended to add snacks like protein bars and trail mix which can give you an extra boost of energy during times of stress. All these foods should have a long shelf life so they don't expire quickly and can last up to several years if stored properly!

Furthermore, it's advisable to bring enough water too. You'll need at least one gallon per person per day for drinking, cooking and sanitation purposes. Tips for Storing and Rotating Emergency Food Supplies


Having an emergency food supply is essential for your hurricane survival kit! (It's) important to plan ahead and make sure you have enough food on hand in case of evacuation. First, it's a good idea to stock up on non-perishable items like canned goods, dry pasta, and rice. You should also consider purchasing freeze-dried meals as they are easy to prepare with only hot water, and can store for a long time.

Moreover, when storing your emergency food supplies it’s crucial to rotate them regularly (so) they don’t expire or become stale. A great way to do this is by writing the expiration date on each item so that you remember which ones need replacing first. It’s also beneficial not to forget about the items at the back of the cupboard - move them around frequently! Additionally, make sure you keep all of your supplies in airtight containers or heavy-duty bags. This will help preserve their freshness while keeping out critters and pests!

In conclusion, when getting ready for hurricane season it’s essential to have an emergency food supply!
