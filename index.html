Frequently Asked Questions

What type of food should be stored for hurricane emergencies?
Non-perishable items such as canned goods, dried foods, and other shelf-stable products are best for hurricane emergency food supplies.
How long can food be safely stored for a hurricane emergency?
Most non-perishable foods can be safely stored for up to two years if kept in optimal conditions, such as at room temperature and away from sunlight and moisture.
What are the best containers to store food for a hurricane emergency?
Plastic or metal containers with airtight lids are ideal for storing food during an emergency situation like a hurricane.
How often should the food supply be checked and rotated?
Its important to check your food supply regularly and rotate it every three to six months to ensure that it stays safe and edible during an emergency situation like a hurricane.
What is Huffman Food Storage?
Huffman Food Storage is an innovative system of pre-packaged meal kits designed specifically for disaster relief situations like hurricanes. The meals have been designed to provide essential nutrition while being easy to prepare and store in limited space, making them ideal for those preparing an emergency food supply kit.
