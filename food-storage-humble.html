Frequently Asked Questions

What food should be kept in a hurricane emergency food supply kit?
Canned and dried goods with a long shelf-life, such as canned vegetables, fruits, beans and tuna; protein bars; nuts; peanut butter; crackers; cereal; powdered milk.
How long can these foods last in storage?
Depending on the type of food, it can last anywhere from several months to several years.
Where is the best place to store emergency food supplies?
A cool, dark place away from moisture and extreme temperatures such as a basement or garage.
What should I do if my emergency food supply has been compromised by floodwaters or other damage?
Discard any items that have had contact with water or debris and replace them with new items as soon as possible.
